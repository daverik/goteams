Welcome to LagVarvet!

Planning to run Göteborgsvarvet? LagVarvet allows you and your friends to run together as a team for the race. By running the race you collect points for your team and compete against other teams. Are you up for the challenge of becoming the best team of Göteborgsvarvet?

213 days left to the race.

SUPPORT

Want to raise awareness for a cause? Run for a charity, or simply support your hometown or your favourite team.

SPECTATE

Know some friend who participate in the race? LagVarvet allows you to view any person's or team's progress during the race via an interactive map.

MOTIVATE

Create a team and invite your friends to run together. Get motivation from your friends to help you reach your goals and inspire other people to reach theirs. LagVarvet lets you share your results and goals for your friends to see.

Afraid you might let your team down by running slowly? Don't worry, our point system ensures you always contribute something to your team, no matter how slow you run.

COMPETE

Feeling competitive? Sign up your team in a league and compete for the top place.
